🎯 How To Play - Solo Mode

×

🎮 Objective

Test your memory by repeating increasingly complex color sequences. See how far you can go!

📋 Rules

  • Watch the sequence of colors that light up
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the buttons in the same order
  • Each round adds one more color to the sequence
  • Make a mistake and the game ends
  • Your high score is saved automatically

🏆 Scoring

Your score increases with each successful round. Challenge yourself to beat your high score!

⚔️ How To Play - 2 Players Duel

×

🎮 Objective

Battle head-to-head! Take turns repeating sequences. Last player standing wins!

📋 Rules

  • Players take turns attempting the same sequence
  • If a player makes a mistake, they're eliminated
  • The sequence gets longer each round that both players survive
  • Last player remaining wins the duel
  • Winner gets bragging rights!

🏆 Victory

Outlast your opponent by maintaining perfect accuracy. One mistake and you're out!

🔺 How To Play - 3 Players Battle

×

🎮 Objective

Triple threat! Three players compete in the ultimate memory challenge. Survive to be the champion!

📋 Rules

  • All three players attempt the same sequence in turn order
  • Players are eliminated when they make a mistake
  • Surviving players continue to the next round with a longer sequence
  • Game continues until only one player remains
  • If multiple players make it through, the sequence increases

🏆 Victory

Be the last player standing in this intense three-way memory battle!

💀 How To Play - Fatal 4-Way

×

🎮 Objective

Four players enter, one player leaves! The ultimate test of memory and nerves.

📋 Rules

  • Four players take turns in sequence order
  • Each player must repeat the exact color sequence
  • One mistake eliminates you from the competition
  • Survivors advance to longer, more challenging sequences
  • The tension builds as players are eliminated one by one

🏆 Victory

Survive the chaos and prove you have the ultimate memory. Fatal 4-Way champion!
